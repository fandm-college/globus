# Monitoring Transfers

Once you click **Start**, Globus submits your transfer request immediately.

Unlike copying files with Windows Explorer or Finder, you do not need to keep your browser open or remain logged in. Globus performs the transfer in the background and automatically retries temporary network interruptions when possible.

When the transfer finishes, Globus automatically sends an email indicating whether the transfer completed successfully or failed.

{: .good-to-know }
> You can safely close your browser after starting a transfer. Globus tracks the transfer independently and emails you when it completes.

## Check transfer status

To monitor transfers before the email arrives:

1. Click **Activity** from the navigation menu on the left.
2. Review active, completed, and failed transfers.
3. Click any transfer for additional details.

## View transfer details

Selecting a transfer displays details such as:

- Source and destination collections
- Transfer status
- Submission and completion times
- Number of files and directories transferred
- Amount of data transferred
- Transfer speed
- Transfer settings
