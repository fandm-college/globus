# Logging In to Globus

## At a glance

- Visit [https://app.globus.org](https://app.globus.org).
- Use the **organizational login**.
- Search for **Franklin & Marshall College**.
- Sign in using your F&M NetID.
- Begin using the Globus web application.

Before you can transfer or share research data, sign in to Globus using your Franklin & Marshall College account.

Globus does **not** require a separate username and password for F&M users. Instead, it uses your Franklin & Marshall credentials through the organizational login.

## Open the Globus login page

Navigate to:

[https://app.globus.org](https://app.globus.org)

You will be presented with the organizational login page.

![Globus organizational login page]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/screenshots/globus-login.png){: .figure }

<div class="figure-caption"><strong>Figure 1.</strong> The Globus organizational login page.</div>

## Select Franklin & Marshall College

Begin typing **Franklin** in the organizational login search box.

Select **Franklin & Marshall College**, then click **Continue**.

![Searching for Franklin & Marshall College in Globus]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/screenshots/globus-org-search-fm.png){: .figure }

<div class="figure-caption"><strong>Figure 2.</strong> Search for and select Franklin & Marshall College before clicking Continue.</div>

After clicking **Continue**, you may be directed to the Franklin & Marshall RapidIdentity login page. Sign in using your F&M NetID and password.

{: .recommendation }
> Always sign in using the **organizational login** and select **Franklin & Marshall College**. Do not use the Google option under **Login using less common options** unless Research Computing Services specifically instructs you to do so.

## After you sign in

After signing in, Globus opens your workspace. Most users will initially see the **Dashboard**, which provides a summary of recent activity and notifications.

![Globus dashboard after logging in]({{ site.baseurl }}/assets/images/screenshots/globus-dashboard.png){: .figure }

<div class="figure-caption"><strong>Figure 3.</strong> The Globus Dashboard after signing in.</div>

The navigation menu on the left provides access to major Globus features, including:

- **File Manager** — Browse collections and transfer files.
- **Activity** — Monitor current and completed transfers.
- **Collections** — Browse or search storage collections.
- **Groups** — Manage collaborator groups.
- **Timers** — Schedule or monitor recurring transfers.
- **Settings** — Review identities and account information.

{: .good-to-know }
> Throughout this guide, we focus primarily on File Manager, Activity, Collections, Groups, Timers, and Settings. Other Globus features are intended for more specialized workflows and are outside the scope of this guide.
